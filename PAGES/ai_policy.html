AI Usage Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2025

1. Responsible AI Usage

We utilize Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to enhance user experience, provide insights, and automate certain tasks. We are committed to using AI in a transparent, ethical, and responsible manner.

2. Data Collection

Our AI systems may process anonymized user data to improve the accuracy and relevance of results. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

3. Human Oversight

While AI can assist in decision-making, all critical decisions are supervised by human operators. AI is used as a support tool, not a replacement for human judgment.

4. Transparency

When content or decisions are generated by AI, we aim to clearly indicate that such content is AI-generated or influenced.

5. Limitations

AI is not perfect. There may be errors or unintended outputs. Users are encouraged to verify AI-generated content, especially in sensitive or critical use cases.

6. User Consent

By using features powered by AI on our site, you consent to the processing of data as described in this policy.

7. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about our AI practices, contact us at ai-support@example.com.
